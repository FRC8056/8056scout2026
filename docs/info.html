Quick Start Guide

Match Scouting

Navigate to the Scout page from the dashboard. Enter the match and team number, then follow the sections to record robot performance during Auto, Teleop, and Endgame.

Pit Scouting

Use the Pit Scout feature to record technical data about a robot while in the pits. This data is invaluable for strategy planning and identifying alliance partners.

Analysis

Visit the Matches page to see all recorded data. You can expand matches to see score breakdowns from the API and detailed reports from our scouts.

Pro Tips

  • Searching for a team on the Teams page will automatically highlight their matches on the Matches page.
  • The Auto Climb rating specifically tracks robot stability and success in the first few seconds.
  • Check for the tick to identify reports from verified team members.
