Quick Start Guide

Match Scouting

Navigate to the Scout page from the dashboard. Enter match/team numbers to record performance during Auto, Teleop, and Endgame.

Pit Scouting

Use the Pit Scout feature to record technical data about a robot in the pits. Invaluable for strategy planning.

Pro Tips
