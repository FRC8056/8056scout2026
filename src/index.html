Quick Start Guide

Match Scouting

Navigate to the Scout page from the dashboard. Enter match/team numbers to record robot performance during Auto, Teleop, and Endgame.

Pit Scouting

Use the Pit Scout feature to record technical data about a robot in the pits. Invaluable for strategy planning.

Pro Tips

  • Searching for a team on the Teams page highlights their matches globally.
  • Check for the blue checkmark to identify verified team members.

Additional Information

Authentication Policy

Verification requires a weekly secret code. Reach out to 8056 Laissez Faire members for access.

Data Security

All data (excluding passwords) is visible to admins for verification and editing.
